About

I’m Adam Noffsinger, a Senior Product Designer at Dropbox working on Design Systems. I’ve been with company since late 2017.

Before joining Dropbox, I was an early designer at Uber and worked on the consumer-facing Rides and Eats mobile apps for about 3 years. It was one heck of an experience.

Before getting into product design, I dabbled in creative writing (poetry, specifically) and was an Account Manager in advertising. Needless to say, it wasn;t for me.

I'm most passionate about mobile product, but have extensive experience across web and desktop as well. I'm interested in solving problems related to transportation and mapping, fitness, gaming, music, and travel. I love getting my hands dirty in code and mentoring new designers.
